The Journey

From the serene landscapes of Himachal Pradesh to the pulse of the Hyderabad nightlife scene, Saurabh Kumar Dhiman has established himself as a premier open-format DJ dedicated to high-energy, genre-transcending musical experiences.

A professionally trained artist from the prestigious Panache The DJ School, I specialize in the art of crowd-reading and seamless multi-genre mixing. My technical expertise and versatility allow me to curate bespoke sonic journeys for clubs, festivals, and elite private events.
